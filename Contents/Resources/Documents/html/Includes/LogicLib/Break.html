${Break}

Breaks a block of statements.

Syntax

${Break}

Examples

Simple example

${For} $1 1 10
    ${Break}
    MessageBox MB_OK "This will never show"
${Next}

In combination with a MessageBox

${For} $1 1 10
    ${IfCmd} MessageBox MB_YESNO "We're at $1, continue up to 10?" IDYES ${||} ${Break} ${|}
${Next}
